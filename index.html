Welcome to Our Coffee Shop!

Discover a world of delightful coffee experiences at our cozy coffee shop. From rich espresso to creamy lattes, we have a blend for every coffee lover.

As it's thought that coffee originated in Ethiopia, it's also believed it made its way north, across the red sea into Yemen in the 15th Century. It then started to be grown here in the Yemeni district of Arabia, and by the 16th century it was known in Persia, Egypt, Syria, and Turkey.

Here are some other facts about the history of coffee:.

  1. Coffee is actually a fruit.
  2. The first people to roast and grind coffee beans were the Arabs, around 1000 years after Christ.
  3. According to legend, an Ethiopian goat herder named Kaldi discovered coffee
  4. Coffee has been banned several times throughout history, including in Mecca in 1511.
  5. The four main types of coffee are Arabica, Robusta, Excelsa, and Liberica.
  6. Brazil has been the world's largest coffee producer for over 150 years.
Our journey from bean to cup started in year 2000. Café culture had not set its footprint and coffee was still considered a luxury. Our quest to provide a true coffee experience and brew a perfect cup led us to create the brand Barista. Spearheading the Indian café culture, Barista is an opportunity to discover both the coffee and the craft.

Today, as we continue to brew your favorite coffee and take time to enhance customer experience, we have created coffee relationship with our patrons in 450+ cafes and over 147+ cities.
